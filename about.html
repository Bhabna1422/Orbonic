About Us

All across the globe our name 'Orbonic Solution' shines bright in textile as well as handicraft industry. We excel in both the fields of work, the credit of this goes to our skilled team designers, artisans and craftsmen. They make our job easier and support us tremendously in coming up with a marvelous collection of textile as well as handcrafted products. This talented team of professionals is led by our mentor, Mr. Ashutosh Pati. He always guides us and manages our company in the most impressive manner. He motivates us to perform in the best way possible, so that we can become the leading manufacturer and exporter of the domains we trade in.

Under the brand name 'Orbonic Garment', we offer a beguiling collection of Mens Denim Jeans, Mens Designer Shirts, Women Jeggings and many other textile creations. Further, 'Orbonic Handicraft' is the brand name that we use for our handcrafted assortment including artistic Brass Handicrafts, Ganesh Statues and much more.
